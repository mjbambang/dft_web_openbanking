OPEN BANKING이란 무엇인가?

Open Banking is a platform-based business approach in which
data, processes, and business functionalities are made available in an ecosystem of
banks, customers, third-party developers, FinTech companies, and partners.
The services provided are financial and may come from banks as well as from third parties.

With the development of the Internet and shared technology, businesses utilizing networks, rather than physical and technical ownership of services and goods, have become the center of new industries, and the platform on which they are based is becoming important
